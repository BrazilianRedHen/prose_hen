TI Adaptive Boundary Element Methods A Posteriori Error Estimators,
   Adaptivity, Convergence, and Implementation
SO ARCHIVES OF COMPUTATIONAL METHODS IN ENGINEERING
AB This paper reviews the state of the art and discusses very recent mathematical developments in the field of adaptive boundary element methods. This includes an overview of available a posteriori error estimates as well as a state-of-the-art formulation of convergence and quasi-optimality of adaptive mesh-refining algorithms.
