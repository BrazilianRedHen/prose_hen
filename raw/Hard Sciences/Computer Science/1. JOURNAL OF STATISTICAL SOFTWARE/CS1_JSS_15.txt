TI extRemes 2.0: An Extreme Value Analysis Package in R
SO JOURNAL OF STATISTICAL SOFTWARE
AB This article describes the extreme value analysis (EVA) R package extRemes version 2.0, which is completely redesigned from previous versions. The functions primarily provide utilities for implementing univariate EVA, with a focus on weather and climate applications, including the incorporation of covariates, as well as some functionality for assessing bivariate tail dependence.
